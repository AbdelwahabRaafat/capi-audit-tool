Common Questions

Why are these "starting from" prices and not fixed prices?
CAPI complexity varies significantly. A simple Shopify store with standard events is very different from a custom headless commerce build with a CRM pipeline. We scope every project before quoting to avoid overcharging simple implementations or underquoting complex ones.
Is the free audit actually free? Are there upsells?
Yes — always free, no account required, no credit card, no hidden upsell inside the audit itself. If you want us to fix the issues we find, that's a separate conversation. Many users just take the report and implement it themselves.
How long does implementation take?
Most CAPI implementations are complete within 3–7 business days, depending on your tech stack and whether we need dev access or a staging environment.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, within 7 days of project start if we haven't begun technical work. Once implementation is underway, we offer credits toward other services.
Can I start with just the audit and decide later?
Absolutely. That's the intended journey. Take the free audit, understand your gaps, then reach out only if you need help fixing them.
