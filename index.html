How CAPI Fixes It

From broken signals to
crystal-clear attribution

CAPI bypasses the browser entirely — a direct, server-to-server line from your business to Meta.

01
Customer Clicks Your Ad
They land on your website. Your server quietly assigns them a unique identifier — not dependent on cookies or browser permissions.
02
Conversion Happens
They complete a purchase, fill a form, or visit your store. Your server captures this — not the browser.
03
Direct Line to Meta
Your server fires an API call directly to Meta. Ad blockers, iOS opt-outs, Safari ITP — none of them can intercept a server-to-server call. Meta attributes the sale.
⚠️ Without CAPI
  • Browser fires Pixel → blocked by iOS, ad-blockers, ITP
  • 40% of conversions attributed on a good day
  • EMQ score: 2–4 (poor match quality)
  • Algorithm optimizes for partial data
  • Offline & CRM conversions: invisible
  • Duplicate events inflate conversion count
VS
✅ With CAPI + Enrichment
  • Server fires API → reaches Meta 100% of the time
  • 85–95% of conversions attributed accurately
  • EMQ score: 8.8–9.3 (elite range)
  • Algorithm has real signal → better audiences
  • Offline & CRM data flows through CAPI
  • Perfect deduplication preserves data integrity
